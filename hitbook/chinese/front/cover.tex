\begin{cabstract}
针对非线性系统瞬态性能难以直接设计以及实物平台易受未知非线性、外界扰动和输入饱和影响的问题，本文以滑模反步法为基础，针对一类严格反馈不确定非线性系统提出了三种预设阻尼比改进方法，并进行了控制器设计、稳定性分析、数值仿真和实物实验验证。

首先，引入二阶类李雅普诺夫稳定性判据，该判据证明若二阶李雅普诺夫不等式成立，则系统输出以近似预设的阻尼比稳定跟踪目标信号，并给出了预设阻尼比的控制器参数设置规则。基于该判据，提出预设阻尼比滑模反步控制方法，设计积分滑模面、虚拟控制函数，构造二阶李雅普诺夫不等式，使系统能够按照参数设置规则实现近似预设阻尼比。进一步，针对实物平台主导未知非线性较强的问题，引入神经网络估计主要未知非线性并实时补偿，并基于李雅普诺夫稳定性判据对闭环系统进行稳定性分析。数值仿真和平台实验表明，引入神经网络后，不同阻尼比对应的上升过程、峰值高低和恢复过程差异能够更加清晰地表现出来。

为提升系统的抗干扰能力和动态恢复品质，提出了基于二阶指令滤波的抗扰预设阻尼比滑模反步控制方法。该方法将高阶导数、非线性系统不确定性和外界扰动统一为广义扰动项，并利用二阶指令滤波器进行补偿。通过设计虚拟控制函数和控制输入，构造出二阶李雅普诺夫不等式，使系统能够按照参数设置规则实现近似预设阻尼比，并基于李雅普诺夫稳定性判据对闭环系统进行稳定性分析。数值仿真和平台实验表明，该方法在受扰后的恢复时间和波动抑制方面均优于未加二阶指令滤波的情形。

针对输入饱和会破坏系统跟踪性能的问题，提出了抗饱和预设阻尼比滑模反步控制方法。将抗饱和补偿项引入控制律进行补偿；通过设计虚拟控制函数和实际控制输入，构造出二阶李雅普诺夫不等式，使系统能够按照参数设置规则保持近似预设阻尼比，并基于李雅普诺夫稳定性判据对闭环系统进行稳定性分析。控制输入饱和条件下的对比实验表明，该方法能够减弱输入饱和对系统动态过程的破坏，使系统在输入饱和条件下仍保持一定的阻尼比特征。数值仿真和平台实验进一步验证了所提方法的可行性和有效性。

\end{cabstract}

\begin{eabstract}
To address the difficulty of directly designing transient performance for nonlinear systems and the influence of unknown nonlinearities, external disturbances, and input saturation on real platforms, this thesis takes sliding-mode backstepping as the framework and proposes three improved methods with predefined damping ratios for a class of uncertain nonlinear strict-feedback systems. Controller design, stability analysis, numerical simulations, and experimental validation are carried out.

First, a second-order Lyapunov-like stability criterion is introduced. This criterion proves that, if the second-order Lyapunov inequality holds, the system output can stably track the target signal with an approximately predefined damping ratio, and parameter selection rules for predefined damping-ratio design are also provided. Based on this criterion, a predefined-damping-ratio sliding-mode backstepping control method is proposed. By designing an integral sliding surface and virtual control laws, a second-order Lyapunov inequality is constructed so that the system can achieve an approximately predefined damping ratio according to the parameter selection rules. Furthermore, to deal with the strong dominant unknown nonlinearities on the real platform, a neural network is introduced to estimate the major unknown nonlinearities and compensate for them in real time, and the stability of the closed-loop system is analyzed based on the Lyapunov stability criterion. Numerical simulations and platform experiments show that, after the neural network is introduced, the differences in the rise process, peak magnitude, and recovery process corresponding to different damping ratios can be revealed more clearly.

Second, to improve the disturbance-rejection capability and dynamic recovery performance of the system, an anti-disturbance predefined-damping-ratio sliding-mode backstepping control method based on a second-order command filter is proposed. In this method, higher-order derivatives, nonlinear-system uncertainties, and external disturbances are unified into a generalized disturbance term and compensated by the second-order command filter. By designing virtual control laws and the control input, a second-order Lyapunov inequality is constructed so that the system can achieve an approximately predefined damping ratio according to the parameter selection rules, and the stability of the closed-loop system is analyzed based on the Lyapunov stability criterion. Numerical simulations and platform experiments show that this method is superior to the case without anti-disturbance compensation in terms of recovery time after disturbances and oscillation suppression.

Third, to address the problem that input saturation may deteriorate the tracking performance of the system, an anti-saturation predefined-damping-ratio sliding-mode backstepping control method is proposed. By designing auxiliary saturation dynamics and anti-saturation compensation terms and introducing them into the control law, and by designing virtual control laws and the actual control input, a second-order Lyapunov inequality is constructed so that the system can maintain an approximately predefined damping ratio according to the parameter selection rules, and the stability of the closed-loop system is analyzed based on the Lyapunov stability criterion. Comparative experiments under input-constrained conditions show that this method can alleviate the damage caused by input saturation to the system dynamic process and enable the system to preserve certain damping-ratio characteristics under input limitations. Comprehensive experiments further verify the feasibility and effectiveness of the proposed methods.
\end{eabstract}
