\begin{cabstract}
针对一类严格反馈结构的不确定非线性系统，围绕系统瞬态指标“超调量/阻尼比”难以在现代李雅普诺夫框架下直接设计的问题，本文研究预设阻尼比的滑模反步控制方法。首先引入具有参数选择规则的二阶类李雅普诺夫稳定性判据，将输出误差的收敛过程约束为满足二阶微分不等式，使阻尼比可由控制器参数近似预先指定，从而实现对超调量与响应速度的可调设计。在此基础上，构造包含积分项的滑模面并给出鲁棒反步控制律，在存在未建模动态与外部扰动时证明闭环信号实用稳定且输出具有期望阻尼比。随后，针对系统主要未知非线性，引入神经网络进行在线逼近，并将逼近残差与外部扰动统一交由滑模鲁棒项处理，形成预设阻尼比神经网络滑模反步控制框架。进一步地，为解决反步递推中虚拟控制导数实现困难以及复杂性爆炸问题，本文在基准框架上引入二阶指令滤波抗扰机制，将滤波误差、外部扰动与拟合残差统一并入广义扰动项分析；同时针对执行器幅值受限问题，在控制输入端引入抗饱和补偿动态，构建输入约束下的预设阻尼比控制方法。最后以四旋翼悬浮机平台为实验对象，在上位机、数据采集卡与功率放大器构成的实时控制平台上实现控制算法，并完成固定姿态与时变姿态跟踪验证。结果表明，所提方法能够在不同阻尼比设定下实现可调超调与稳定快速跟踪，其中二阶指令滤波抗扰与抗饱和扩展均表现出较好的工程有效性。
\end{cabstract}

\begin{eabstract}
This thesis studies a preset-damping-ratio sliding-mode backstepping control framework for strict-feedback nonlinear systems with uncertainties, with emphasis on explicitly shaping transient performance through interpretable controller parameters. A second-order Lyapunov method is first introduced to impose a second-order differential inequality on the output error dynamics, so that the damping ratio can be approximately predetermined and the overshoot-response tradeoff can be directly tuned. Based on this criterion, a robust sliding-mode backstepping controller with an integral sliding surface is constructed and analyzed in the presence of unmodeled dynamics and external disturbances. To compensate for the dominant unknown nonlinearities, a neural-network-based approximation scheme is then incorporated into the baseline design, while the residual approximation error and disturbances are handled by the sliding-mode robust term. Furthermore, to avoid the explosion of complexity caused by repeated differentiation of virtual control laws, a second-order command-filtered anti-disturbance mechanism is developed, in which filter errors, external disturbances, and nominal fitting residuals are unified into a generalized disturbance term for stability analysis. In addition, an anti-saturation compensation dynamic is introduced at the actuator side to extend the design to input-constrained scenarios. Experiments on a quadrotor hover system implemented on a real-time PC/DAQ/amplifier platform validate that the proposed framework achieves stable attitude tracking with tunable overshoot and response speed under different preset damping-ratio settings, while the command-filtered anti-disturbance and anti-saturation extensions further improve engineering performance.
\end{eabstract}
